Stress and anxiety is found amongst many students attending a university.

According to BBC, mental health on campus has risen from about 8,000 to 18,000 in the last four years. Studies have been done to examine the stress factors that are causing the students to have anxiety. According to health news, students suffering from stress have an average of a 2.72 GPA while students whom are not suffering from stress have an average of a 3.3 GPA. Research was conducted to acknowledge which stress factor is causing the most anxiety amongst the students at the University of Miami and what can be done to help these individuals control their stress, thus have a better academic performance.

The counseling center at the University of Miami provides free weekly one hour counseling for its students. There are many trained professionals at the center willing to help students deal with any problems they are facing. The center however, does not collect different statistics based on their patients. Dr. Rene Monteagudo mentions how anxiety and stress is the most common cause for students attending the counseling center. What factors are causing the anxiety and stress in our student body? What causes stress?

You have a test on Wednesday, a 30min power point presentation due Wednesday, a report due Thursday, 30 pages to read by each class, a group project due on Friday, 4 classes to attend throughout these deadlines plus your personal life to handle. Stressed yet? Stress is a normal part of life that motivates you to get through each day as long as you take control of your stress. If you can’t handle the stress however, and it becomes long-term, it can interfere with your school, job, personal life and health. According to Health and Benefits, more than half of Americans say they fight with friends and loved ones because of stress.

Want to grab a drink? This is the phase many use when they are stressed out and want to get away from their current situation in life. Within our survey, a correlation was made between stress and alcohol consumption.

Consequences Stress May Cause

Stress appears differently in each individual. If your body can’t handle a physical or emotional threat, your body releases stress hormones such as cortisol. A constant increase of cortisol increases your body to be at risk for anxiety, depression, mood swings, insomnia, over eating, failing grades, and others. Anxiety is the most common disorder found amongst our students. The more stress a person undergoes, the more cortisol is released throughout the body causing a disruption in one’s quality of life.

Counseling Center May Help You

The University of Miami offers free counseling to all its students. A 45 min session can be scheduled once a week with a trained professional. A Psychologist is on call 24/7 for any emergency calls. According to the University of Miami’s counseling center, anxiety was the most common factor found amongst the students. For this reason they offer group therapy and ways to handle anxiety through therapy. Within our survey, 86% of the students mentioned they suffer from anxiety however only 8% of the students attend the counseling center.
